Blown-In Insulation

The right choice for attics, retrofits, and irregular spaces. Fast install, no demolition, immediate energy savings.

Blown-in insulation adapts to fit spaces traditional batts can't reach — attic floors with awkward framing, walls with hidden cavities, irregular nooks around HVAC and plumbing. We use fiberglass, cellulose, and foam-blend products to create a thermal barrier with no gaps.

When blown-in is the right call

Blown-in is usually our recommendation when:

  • Your attic just needs more — adding R-value over existing insulation is fast and cost-effective.
  • The space is irregular — older homes with non-standard framing, knee walls, or weird angles.
  • You want a retrofit without demo — blown-in fills wall cavities through small holes, no drywall removal needed.
  • Budget matters — typically less expensive per square foot than spray foam, while still cutting your bills significantly.

Materials we use

  • Cellulose — recycled paper treated for fire and pest resistance. Excellent thermal performance, sustainable, and great for older homes.
  • Fiberglass — proven, affordable, won't settle significantly. Best for new construction and attic top-ups.
  • Hybrid systems — sometimes a blown-in attic plus spray foam at the rim joists is the best value. We'll tell you when that's the case.

What to expect on install day

Most attic-only jobs are completed in a single day. We protect floors and contents, blow the material in to the specified R-value, and verify depth across the entire space. The mess stays in the attic, not in your living room.

Service area

We work across Lakeland, Tampa, Plant City, Wesley Chapel, Orlando, and the rest of Central Florida. Residential, commercial, factories, warehouses, barndominiums — we've insulated them all.

Service quick facts

  • Most attics done in one day
  • No drywall demo for wall retrofits
