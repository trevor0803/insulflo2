Storm Damage Repair

When storms compromise your insulation, every day you wait makes it worse. We assess fast, document for your insurance, and restore your home.

When storms damage your home's insulation, it's critical to act quickly to prevent further issues like mold, moisture damage, and energy loss. We provide comprehensive storm damage insulation repair and replacement services across Central Florida.

What storm damage to insulation actually looks like

  • Wind-driven rain through roof or soffit damage — saturates attic insulation, which loses R-value the moment it gets wet.
  • Roof leaks that develop after a storm — sometimes don't show in your ceiling for weeks. Insulation hides the early signs.
  • Wind-displaced insulation — high winds through attic vents can move blown-in material into piles, leaving large unprotected areas.
  • Mold and mildew growth — starts within 48 hours of wet insulation in Florida humidity.
  • Compromised vapor barriers — even if the insulation looks okay, the membrane behind it might not be.

What we do

Our certified, licensed, and insured team assesses the damage, documents everything with photos for your insurance claim, removes affected material, and restores your home's insulation to optimal performance.

As a family-owned company, every job is handled personally by our team — ensuring quality workmanship and attention to detail on every project. We restore comfort and energy efficiency to homes after storms, hurricanes, and moisture damage.

Insurance claim help

We work with most major Florida insurers and can provide the documentation, photos, and detailed invoices your adjuster needs. We've seen how these claims work — and we'll tell you upfront what's covered and what isn't.

Why move fast

Insulation damage compounds. Wet material attracts mold, sags, and damages the framing underneath. A small leak ignored for a month can turn into a full attic replacement plus drywall repairs. Most storm jobs we see could have been one-third the cost if the homeowner had called right away.
